How it works in 3 steps

Designed so that anyone can use it in under a minute.

1
Add your content
Paste text directly or upload a PDF, Word, PowerPoint, or text file – the app extracts what it needs automatically.
2
Pick style & length
Choose tone (neutral, academic, casual) and length (short, medium, long). Then select a few summary “methods” to compare.
3
Compare & decide
See all summaries side‑by‑side with simple quality indicators. Keep the one you like, copy it, or try again with different settings.
